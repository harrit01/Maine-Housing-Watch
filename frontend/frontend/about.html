About Maine Housing Watch

Maine Housing Watch is an automated tool that tracks planning board meetings across all 492 Maine municipalities, identifying housing-related proposals in real time.

Why This Matters

Housing proposals often get approved or denied based on who shows up to speak. NIMBYs are organized. Now pro-housing advocates can be too.

How It Works

  • Scrapers check municipal websites hourly for new agendas
  • AI classifies agenda items as housing-related or not
  • You get alerts about meetings where your voice matters
